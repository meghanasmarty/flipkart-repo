About

Flipkart is one of India's largest e-commerce companies, founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. It began as an online bookstore and quickly expanded into multiple categories such as electronics, fashion, home essentials, and groceries, revolutionizing shopping in India.

Career

Careers at Flipkart are filled with innovation, customer focus, and leadership. Flipkart offers roles across engineering, supply chain, analytics, business development, and design.

  • High-impact roles in e-commerce and logistics
  • Growth-focused culture
  • Women empowerment and inclusive hiring
  • Flipkart LEAP – startup innovation program

Education

Founders Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal are alumni of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi, one of India's most prestigious engineering colleges. Their technical backgrounds laid a strong foundation for Flipkart’s tech-driven operations and customer-centric systems.

Family

Flipkart's work culture fosters a sense of family among its employees. The company encourages collaboration, innovation, and well-being. Employees often refer to Flipkart as the “Flipkart Family” due to its employee-first initiatives and vibrant office culture.

Achievements

  • Acquired by Walmart in 2018 for $16 billion
  • Over 400 million registered users
  • Leader in Big Billion Days sale events
  • Launch of PhonePe (later spun off)
  • Consistent ranking among India’s top workplaces
